AirPods won't connect to iPhone

Product: Apple AirPods (all generations) · Platform: iPhone iOS 14+ · Last reviewed: April 2026

Troubleshooting

AirPods do not connect automatically
  • Open Settings on your iPhone and tap Bluetooth.
  • Confirm the Bluetooth toggle is green. If it is already on, toggle it off, wait 5 seconds, then toggle it back on.
  • Place both AirPods in the charging case and open the lid near your iPhone.
    • Checking that a battery card appears on screen — this confirms the AirPods are being detected.
    • If either AirPod shows below 10% battery, charging them for at least 15 minutes before continuing.
  • Tap your AirPods name under My Devices to connect manually if they do not connect on their own.
